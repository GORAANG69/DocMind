About Us

Malhotra Earth Movers and Transports

Welcome to Malhotra Earth Movers and Transports. We are a transportation and earth moving company dedicated to providing reliable, safe, and timely tipper transportation services for construction, infrastructure, mining, excavation, and waste management projects.

Our goal is to deliver efficient logistics solutions while maintaining professionalism, transparency, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to supporting contractors, builders, government agencies, and industrial clients with dependable transportation services.

  • Delhi NCR's trusted tipper fleet
  • Modern Ashok Leyland tipper trucks
  • Safety-first operations & compliance
  • 24/7 responsive customer support
